About Me

Background

I'm a 22 year old programmer based in Melbourne, Australia with a passion for games and over 6 years experience working with Unity and C#. I started making games around 2014 with GameMaker and haven't looked back since! I have a deep passion for programming and I love the incredible problem-solving opportunities it presents and the creativity required to solve them!

My spare time is usually spent playing indie games, participating in game jams and working on side projects which can also be found here as I continue to work on them. I love being able to design and develop fun experiences for others! I believe any problem, no matter how impossible it may seem, can be overcome if you put your mind to it.

Qualifications & Experience

Between 2021 - 2023 I studied at the Academy of Interactive Entertainment, completing the Diploma of Screen and Media, Diploma of Information Technology and Advanced Diploma of Professional Game Development. From 2022-2023 I worked part-time at Monotreme Interactive on their debut game for PC and Nintendo Switch, which is yet to be officially announced.

Currently I'm working on Enter The Chronosphere at Effort Star Games, developing the level generation systems from the ground up, and more. If you'd like to play the game and see my recent work in action, we're currently running an open beta test which is accessible from the Steam page!
